WHACK-A-NOTE
Random-note fingerboard and rhythm trainer from Starlight Ukes.

TIMING
Each session starts with a full one-measure count-in.

Root Mode: A note appears on beat 2. Find it on beats 3-4 and play it on the next beat 1. The reference pitch sounds on beat 1.

Root-Fifth Mode: The first root appears on beat 2 after the count-in. Play the root on the following beat 1 and its fifth on beat 2. For roots below A1, play the fifth above; for A1 and higher, play the fifth below. The next root appears immediately on beat 3, giving you beats 3-4 to prepare for the next pattern.

The metronome uses a wood-block-style sound, with a stronger downbeat.

PRACTICE TIMER
The timer defaults to 3 minutes. Enter 1-120 minutes, or 0 for an unlimited session. Timing begins after the count-in.
