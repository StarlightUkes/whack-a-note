How to Play

Choose a mode

  • Root Mode: A note appears on beat 1. Play that note on beat 4, when the reference tone sounds.
  • Root–Fifth Mode: A root appears on beat 1. Play the root on beat 3, then play the note a perfect fourth below on beat 4. Those two notes form a root–fifth pattern.

Set up your practice

  1. Choose the lowest and highest notes you want to practice.
  2. Leave sharps and flats unchecked for natural notes only, or select the accidentals you want to include.
  3. Set a comfortable tempo and choose a practice time. Enter 0 for an unlimited session.
  4. Press Start.
  5. Find each displayed note before its reference tone sounds.
  6. Listen to the reference tone to check your pitch.

Root Mode defaults to G1–C3. Root–Fifth Mode defaults to A1–C3. You can change either range before starting.
